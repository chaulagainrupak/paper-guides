## Displacement, Velocity, and Acceleration

Displacement is the vector from start point to end point. Velocity is the rate of change of displacement. Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity.

## SUVAT Equations

Valid only when acceleration is constant:

- v = u + at
- s = ut + ½at²
- v² = u² + 2as
- s = ½(u + v)t

## Projectile Motion

Horizontal and vertical motion are independent. Horizontal velocity is constant throughout. Vertical acceleration is g = 9.81 m/s² downward.
